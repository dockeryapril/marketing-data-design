The BRANDING Workbook

There is a long, comprehensive, and sometimes daunting list of UX research methods, but at its center is the user and how they think and behave, their needs and motivations. UX research does this through observation techniques, task analysis, and other feedback methodologies.

I remember searching online for books and articles that could just give me a step-by-step guide to help other brands build that experience and connection with their audience. The truth is, there is no ONE magic formula, and there were plenty of guides out there that range from very vague to very expensive. When I couldn't find the material I was looking for, I decided to take everything that I had learned over the years and create the guide I was looking for. This turned into the creation of the first volume of The BRANDING Workboook.
