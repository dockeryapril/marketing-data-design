The Reel Plug Fishing Co.

I absolutely love the outdoors, especially fishing. So I started my own fishing brand. The Reel Plug Fishing Co. provides convenient and affordable fishing gear making fishing accessible for everyone. Developed on Shopify, this platform allows me to build with their lo-fi wireframe templates and interactive prototypes before publishing a live site.

This custom theme offers an engaging and informational user experience:

  • Online Store 2.0 compatible and optimized for speed and performance. Fast page loading gives shoppers instant access to product pages giving them the info they need to make easier and well-informed boat buying desicions
  • Mobile-first design recognizing that fisherfolx are out here on the move and on their phones and tablets more, even for online shopping
  • HTML plugin that offers interactive maps for quickly locating safe places to fish and boat
