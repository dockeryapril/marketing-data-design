About Me

I began my Creative journey 13 years ago, obtaining a degree in Multi-Age Education to become an art teacher and freelance illustrator. I transitioned my creative skills into graphic design and photography. This led to more opportunities to actually utilize my observational skills and limited psychology knowledge, obtained from teaching, into designing user experiences and interfaces for a variety of clients and industries. From designing logos and branding guides for first-time entrepreneurs and small local businesses. To working with the creative briefs of big name consumer brands and running multi-channel campaigns for financial institutions with millions in assets. My experience is solidified with an adaptable and evolving skillset and dedication to design and development principles that give me the confidence to push boundaries and get results.

Developing on Shopify allows me to build on their lo-fi wireframe templates into interactive prototypes to a live site all on one platform, while bridging my training from UX/UI to front-end wed development.
